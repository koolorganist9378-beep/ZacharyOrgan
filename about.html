Our Mission

The Virtual Pipe Organ Project aims to digitally preserve and recreate the sound of historic and modern pipe organs using accessible technology and open-source tools.

What We Do

We sample, document, and digitally simulate real pipe organs so that anyone can explore their tone colors, registrations, and mechanical features from any computer.

Future Goals

  • Build a full-featured virtual console interface
  • Include sample sets from real instruments
  • Collaborate with organ builders and musicians
